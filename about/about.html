STAFF

Moses Michael is a 34-year-old Israeli musician and sound engineer whose life has been defined by an unwavering passion for music. Hailing from Israel, Moses picked up the guitar at the age of 13 and never looked back, carving out a remarkable journey through the worlds of metal and punk. Over the years, he’s lent his talents to numerous bands, including standout acts like Captain Obvious, BBB, and MinDrive. Today, he’s a key member of the progressive metal band Many Faces, where he not only shines as a guitarist but also takes on the critical role of sound engineer. With Many Faces, Moses has already helped release two electrifying singles, showcasing his ability to blend creativity with technical precision.

Driven by a desire to master the art of sound, Moses pursued formal training after years of performing. He enrolled in the prestigious Sound Engineering program at Kinneret College, dedicating two years to honing his craft. Graduating with honors, he earned a professional diploma in Sound Engineering—a testament to his commitment and skill. This education, paired with his deep-rooted experience as a musician, gave him a rare edge: the ability to understand music from both the artist’s and engineer’s perspectives.

For more than five years, Moses has been a force in music production, working tirelessly to bring artists’ visions to life. His expertise spans the full spectrum of audio production—vocal tracking, live instrument recording, DI sessions, editing, vocal tuning, mixing, and mastering. As a producer, he’s known for his collaborative approach, partnering closely with bands and solo artists to ensure every project reflects their unique voice. His meticulous attention to detail and infectious enthusiasm leave every client with a product they’re thrilled to share with the world.

At Loud Devils Studio, Moses channels his love for heavy music and technical prowess into creating recordings that pack a punch. Whether he’s crafting a bone-rattling mix for a metal band, refining vocals for a singer-songwriter, or mastering a track to cut through the noise, Moses brings the same level of dedication to every session. His background as a performer allows him to connect with artists on a creative level, while his engineering skills ensure a polished, professional result.

To Moses, music is a universal language—a powerful way to tell stories and forge connections. His mission is to help artists express their truth with clarity and intensity, transforming their ideas into something unforgettable. Whether you’re a band ready to unleash your debut or an artist seeking to elevate your sound, Moses is the mind and ears you can trust to make your music resonate.

LOUD DEVILS STUDIO

Loud Devils Studio is meticulously crafted to deliver world-class sound. The space is fully acoustically treated to ensure pristine recordings, free from unwanted noise or distortion. Equipped with industry-standard Pro Tools, the studio operates at the cutting edge of audio production, paired with a top-tier sound card and a high-RAM computer for seamless performance. The setup includes two types of professional-grade headphones and ADAM Audio studio monitors, renowned for their clarity and precision, allowing artists and engineers to hear every detail with unmatched accuracy.

The studio is a haven for musicians, stocked with high-end instruments and gear to meet any creative need.

Artists have access to two electric guitars—a PRS and a Schecter—alongside a classical guitar and a Sterling - Music Man bass guitar. For those seeking the perfect tone, the studio offers two guitar amplifiers, a Vox Pathfinder and a Fender Frontman, ready to bring performances to life. This thoughtfully curated selection ensures that musicians can focus on their craft without worrying about equipment.

While Loud Devils Studio thrives in the realms of metal and rock—genres close to Moses’s heart—it’s built to accommodate every style of music and audio production. From vocal tracking and live instrument recordings to DI sessions, editing, vocal tuning, mixing, and mastering, the studio provides end-to-end services tailored to each artist’s vision. Whether you’re a punk band laying down your debut EP, a singer-songwriter crafting intimate acoustic tracks, or a podcaster needing pristine audio, the team at Loud Devils approaches every project with the same passion and precision.

What sets Loud Devils Studio apart is its blend of technical excellence and creative collaboration. Under Moses’s leadership, the studio fosters an environment where artists feel supported and inspired. His dual perspective as a guitarist and engineer allows him to connect with clients on a deeper level, ensuring that every session captures not just sound, but emotion and intent. The result? Recordings that are brutally clear, devilishly loud, and true to the artist’s vision.
