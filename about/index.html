STAFF

Moses Michael is a 34-year-old Israeli musician and sound engineer whose life has been shaped by a deep passion for music. From the moment he picked up a guitar at 13, Moses has been immersed in the creative world of sound, developing his craft through years of performing, recording, and producing across a wide range of genres. Moses’s journey through the music world has been marked by constant learning and evolution. As he honed his skills, he began to explore various aspects of music production, from writing and recording to mixing and mastering. His natural curiosity pushed him to expand his understanding of sound, eventually leading him to study sound engineering. His love for both the emotional and technical sides of music has driven him to constantly seek new ways to grow. Over time, what started as a hobby became a full-fledged calling, fueled by countless hours in rehearsal rooms and studios. Each experience helped shape his identity as a multifaceted artist. His natural curiosity pushed him to expand his understanding of sound, eventually leading him to study sound engineering.

Over the years, he has played and collaborated with numerous bands, including Captain Obvious, BBB, and MinDrive, gaining a reputation for his dynamic playing and sharp ear. Today, he’s a core member of the progressive group Many Faces, where he contributes both as a guitarist and as the band’s sound engineer, helping produce impactful, high-quality recordings.

Eager to deepen his expertise, Moses completed formal studies in sound engineering at Kinneret College, graduating with honors. His technical training, combined with extensive hands-on experience as a musician, gives him a rare ability to approach music from both creative and technical perspectives.

For over five years, Moses has worked with artists from across the musical spectrum—rock, pop, indie, singer-songwriters, electronic acts, and more. His work includes everything from vocal tracking and live instrument recording to editing, vocal tuning, mixing, and mastering. Known for his collaborative style, Moses takes pride in helping artists bring their vision to life with clarity, precision, and emotional impact.

At Loud Devils Studio, Moses blends technical know-how with a musician’s intuition. Whether he’s building a lush mix for a solo artist, tightening a groove-heavy rhythm section, or polishing a track for radio release, he brings dedication, creativity, and a keen ear to every session.

To Moses, music is a universal language—a way to tell stories, inspire emotion, and forge human connection. His mission is to help artists express themselves authentically, and make sure their music sounds as powerful as the message behind it.

LOUD DEVILS STUDIO

Loud Devils Studio is meticulously designed to deliver professional, world-class sound. The acoustically treated space ensures clean, accurate recordings, free from noise or distortion. Powered by industry-standard Pro Tools, a high-performance sound card, and a powerful, high-RAM computer, the studio provides a seamless, efficient workflow for all types of audio production. Artists benefit from premium ADAM Audio studio monitors and multiple types of pro-grade headphones, offering clear, detailed monitoring for every stage of the process. With a focus on both technical precision and creative flexibility, the studio is built to accommodate diverse musical needs, from intricate mixes to high-energy recordings. Every session is supported by top-tier equipment and an environment designed to inspire creativity and collaboration. The studio’s warm, focused atmosphere makes artists feel at ease, encouraging authentic performances and a deep connection to their work.

The studio is a creative hub equipped with a curated selection of high-quality instruments and gear. Musicians have access to multiple guitars—including electric, classical, and bass models—as well as versatile amplifiers that suit a variety of tones and styles. Whether you're recording layered arrangements or stripped-down acoustic sessions, everything is in place to support your sound.

Services include vocal tracking, live instrument recordings, DI sessions, editing, tuning, mixing, and mastering—all customized to meet the unique goals of each artist.
